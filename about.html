Our Story

Founded in 2021 by Dr. Sotiris Kakanos, autoscientific was created with a single, focused mission: to commercialize the ReachingBot, an innovative lab instrument that automates the single pellet reaching task in mice.

The single pellet reaching task is a widely used behavioral assessment in neuroscience research, traditionally requiring manual operation and extensive researcher time. Our ReachingBot transforms this process through precision automation, delivering consistent, reliable results while freeing researchers to focus on their scientific discoveries.

Our Founder

Dr. Sotiris Kakanos

Founder & CEO

Dr. Sotiris Kakanos founded autoscientific in 2021 to bring the ReachingBot technology to market. Recognizing the need for automation in neuroscience behavioral testing, Dr. Kakanos developed this specialized instrument to address the time-intensive and variable nature of manual single pellet reaching tasks. His vision was to create a product that would enhance research reproducibility while allowing scientists to focus on what they do best: scientific discovery.
